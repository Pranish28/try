About Us

Since our establishment in 2006 AD, our hearts have been set on a single, unwavering purpose: To complete the call that God has placed upon this ministry. For two decades, we have stood as a lighthouse in Murgiya, dedicated to transforming lives through the Gospel and nurturing a community that walks in the footsteps of Jesus Christ. We believe that a "calling" isn't just a moment—it is a lifelong commitment to service, worship, and spreading the light of God to every corner of our community.

Our Mission

To glorify God by making disciples, serving our community, and sharing the love of Christ.

Our Community

Join a welcoming congregation where faith, fellowship, and family come together.
