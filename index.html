About Us

Bethany Baptist Church Murgiya is a vibrant community of believers dedicated to spreading the Gospel and serving our neighbors. Founded with a vision to foster spiritual growth and unity, we welcome all who seek a deeper relationship with God.

Our Community

Join a welcoming congregation where faith, fellowship, and family come together.
